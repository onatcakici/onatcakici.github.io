Porter

Autonomous mobile robot

In 2026, I'm building an autonomous indoor delivery robot designed to reduce repetitive delivery work for staff in hotels, residences, and hospitals. Porter combines secure cargo compartments, LiDAR and depth sensing, and ROS 2 navigation for real buildings.

Rescue Robotic Arm

Soft robotics / field deployment

At 16, I helped build a telescoping robotic arm used alongside firefighters to pull a trapped puppy to safety. We heard the news while preparing for a robotics competition and built it within three hours, with a soft gripper for field use.

RangerEye

Autonomous VTOL / wildfire

In 2024-2025, I led a nine-engineer team developing an autonomous VTOL designed to help detect wildfires earlier in remote areas. I worked across autonomy, flight testing, charging, and ground-station integration.
